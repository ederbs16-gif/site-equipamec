How We Do It

From Raw Steel to Finished Equipment

01

Chassis & Metalwork

All structural steel is fabricated in-house using carbon steel. The chassis is engineered to withstand high-vibration environments and marine exposure, with dimensions calculated by our technicians for each model.

02

Structural Welding

MIG/MAG welding performed by certified professionals using Balmer equipment. Every weld point is inspected before moving to the next stage. We don't outsource this step — it defines the equipment's integrity.

03

Machining & Panel

The front panel is milled and drilled with precise tolerances to accommodate gauges, valves and hose fittings. Our machining department lets us fabricate custom parts without relying on external suppliers.

04

Motor Integration

Combustion engines (Branco, Terramar) or electric motors are coupled to the chassis with millimetric alignment. The hydraulic transmission is assembled and adjusted at this stage, ensuring pressure and flow meet specs before closing the unit.

05

Lathe & Custom Parts

Parts that don't exist in the market or need specific adaptation are turned in-house. This allows us to fulfill custom requests without supplier lead times or import costs.

06

Testing & Delivery

Before leaving the factory, every unit goes through the Test Center, where it is operated under real load. Pressure, flow, temperature and sealing are verified. The equipment is only released when it meets project specifications.
