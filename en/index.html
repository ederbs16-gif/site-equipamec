Who We Are

Precision Engineering. Power that Moves the Future.

Brazilian pioneer in hydraulic technology and complex metallurgical solutions.

Headquartered in Caraguatatuba – SP, Equipamec Soluções has consolidated itself as a strategic force in the naval, port, offshore, and industrial sectors. More than manufacturing equipment, we develop intelligence applied to engineering.

We are absolute pioneers in Brazil in developing Combustion-Powered Hydraulic Units with Proprietary Technology. Our innovation in electric and combustion units was born from the need to deliver energy independence and maximum efficiency for operations in extreme environments, where reliability is not an option, but a rule.
