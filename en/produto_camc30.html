C.A.M.C/30 — Combustion Air Compressor

The C.A.M.C/30 is Equipamec's combustion-driven air compressor, engineered to deliver high-flow compressed air with stable pressure in operations requiring full energy autonomy — with no dependency on electrical grid supply. Powered by a 13HP combustion engine with a flow rate of 485 LPM and working pressure of 14.2 kgf/cm², it is the ideal solution for surface-supplied diving operations, vessel decks, shipyards, offshore platforms and remote areas where electrical infrastructure is unavailable or unreliable. The pressure vessel is manufactured from ASTM A516/60 steel, ASME VIII-VIV1 standard, ensuring full compliance with NR13C/5/V safety regulations.
