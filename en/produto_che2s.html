Electric Hydraulic Units

CHE2S — 25HP Electric

The CHE2S 25HP is a high-performance electric hydraulic unit, created by Equipamec for use in industries, ports and field operations that demand continuous power, compact size and lower operating costs. Equipped with a 25HP electric motor, it eliminates fuel dependency, providing quieter, cleaner and more economical operation wherever electrical power is available. With two independent hydraulic outlets of 40 LPM each, it allows two work fronts to operate simultaneously with full pressure stability.

Technical Specifications

Outlets2 independent outlets
Flow/Outlet2×40 liters/min

Pressure100 bar (average)

Key Features

  • Electric motor — silent, clean, zero emissions operation
  • Two independent outlets for simultaneous operation
  • Stable 100 bar pressure on both outlets
  • 120L hydraulic tank — extended operational autonomy
  • Compact dimensions — easy installation and positioning
  • Fixed carbon steel skid — robust and durable structure
  • Lower maintenance cost compared to combustion engines
  • Zero fuel consumption — reduced operating cost
