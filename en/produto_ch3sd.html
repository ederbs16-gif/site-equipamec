CH3SD — 27HP Diesel

Designed for high-performance hydraulic applications, the Equipamec CH3SD-27HP delivers continuous power output, extended operational autonomy, and the capability to operate simultaneously across three independent work fronts with full pressure stability. Its 27HP three-cylinder diesel engine provides high torque, fuel efficiency, and superior operational reliability, making it ideal for marine, port, industrial, and field applications.
