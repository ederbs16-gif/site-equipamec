Hull Cleaning Machines

HPJ-40 — Hull Polisher

The HPJ-40 is a high-performance hydraulic cleaning machine developed by Equipamec for subsea hull surface cleaning operations on vessels. Equipped with hydraulic motors and a 304L stainless steel housing, the HPJ-40 is designed to withstand the most aggressive environments in the naval, port, and offshore industries. Its orbital motion system ensures a uniform and efficient finish over large surface areas, significantly reducing operation time.

Technical Specifications

MotorOrbital Hydraulic Motor
Operating Pressure110 bar (average)

Key Features

  • Orbital hydraulic motors — uniform coverage over large surfaces
  • Stainless steel housing and flange — full corrosion resistance
  • Dry and underwater operation
  • 110 bar / 40 LPM — continuous, stable power output
  • 800×350mm dimensions — wide coverage per pass
  • Ideal for hull cleaning and surface treatment
  • Significant reduction in operational time over large areas

  • Low maintenance cost
