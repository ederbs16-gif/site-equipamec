PHJ-20 — Manual Hydraulic Polisher

The PHJ-20 is a high-performance manual hydraulic polisher, developed by Equipamec for industrial and underwater applications that demand power, precision and continuous operation in harsh environments. With a maximum speed of 3,500 RPM and 1.8 kW of power, it delivers constant, stable torque even under load — outperforming conventional electric polishers in durability and operational safety.

Technical Specifications

Max. Speed3,500 RPM
Power1.8 kW
Oil Flow20 L/min
Pressure110 bar
Disc FixingVelcro / Backing plate
HandleAdjustable
Weight4 kg
Compatible DiscsG-24, N-V, P-L, G-36, D-AB

Key Features

  • 3,500 RPM — professional-grade finish
  • Safe in wet and submerged environments
  • 5 interchangeable disc types
  • Quick-change velcro/backing plate system
  • Adjustable handle — full ergonomic control
  • Only 4 kg — light for extended use
  • Low maintenance cost
