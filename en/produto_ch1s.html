Combustion Hydraulic Units

CH1S — 25HP

Developed for high-performance hydraulic applications in marine, port, and industrial environments, the Equipamec CH1S 25HP delivers continuous power and operational reliability in a compact, easy-to-move structure. Equipped with a 25HP Branco gasoline engine, the unit provides 40 L/min flow and average pressure of 100 bar, ensuring consistent hydraulic circuit performance throughout the entire hose length of up to 120 meters.

Outlets1 independent outlet
