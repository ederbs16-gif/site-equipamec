Combustion Hydraulic Units

CH2S — 35HP

Designed for high-demand hydraulic applications, the Equipamec CH2S 35HP delivers consistent flow, pressure stability, and the ability to operate two tools simultaneously. Engineered to support hydraulic hoses up to 120 meters, it maintains torque and rotational performance under continuous load, ensuring operational reliability across marine, offshore, and industrial environments.

Technical Specifications

EngineBranco 35HP Gasoline
Outlets2 independent outlets
Flow/Outlet40 liters/min
Total Flow80 L/min (simultaneous)
Pressure100 bar (average)
HoseUp to 120 meters
Oil Tank80 liters
Dimensions700 × 900 mm
CouplingQuick-connect fittings
SkidCarbon steel + wheels, triple-layer coating
Control PanelGauges + Hour meter
Weight300 kg
CoolingForced bilateral ventilation

Key Features

  • 2 independent diving circuits operating simultaneously
  • 120m hose — no critical pressure drop
  • Individual pressure gauges per outlet
  • Hour meter for preventive maintenance
  • Forced bilateral ventilation
  • Wheeled skid — easy deck mobility
  • Low maintenance cost
