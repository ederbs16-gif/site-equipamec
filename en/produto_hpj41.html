Hull Cleaning Machines

HPJ-41 — Small Hull Polisher

The HPJ-41 is the compact version of Equipamec's Hull Polisher line, designed for hull cleaning, polishing and surface treatment in restricted-access areas and operations requiring greater maneuverability — both dry and underwater. Weighing only 25 kg and featuring integrated buoyancy floats, the HPJ-41 achieves neutral weight underwater — completely eliminating physical strain on the operator during underwater work.
