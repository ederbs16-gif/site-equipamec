Brushes & Accessories

ECA Line — Hull Cleaning Brushes

ECA Brushes are hull cleaning and treatment accessories developed by Equipamec for use with the HPJ-40 and HPJ-41 orbital hydraulic polishers. Available in two product lines: ECA/G.M.F (carbon steel bristles in three gauges) and ECA SOFT (soft cable/nylon bristles), covering applications from heavy incrustation removal to surface polishing and finishing. Both versions use a marine plywood disc, feature a 380 mm diameter, and operate at 1,700 RPM max.

Maximum Speed1,700 RPM
DiscMarine Plywood
Diameter380 mm

Key Features

  • Two complementary lines — ECA/G.M.F (steel) + ECA SOFT (nylon)
  • Full operation coverage — from heavy cleaning to fine polishing
  • Compatible with HPJ-40 and HPJ-41
  • Marine plywood disc
  • ECA/G.M.F: 3 wire gauges (F/G/M)
  • ECA SOFT: nylon cable — polishing without surface damage
  • Dry and underwater operation
  • Domestic manufacturing — quick replacement and accessible cost
