MHTP — Thermoplastic Hydraulic Hose Bundle

The MHTP Hydraulic Hose Bundle is the hose assembly developed by Equipamec to integrate the hydraulic circuit of polishers and tools with the diver's air supply in a single operational line. The assembly consists of three hoses: two thermoplastic hydraulic hoses (5/8" pressure and 3/4" return) and one OFF SHORE/DIVE NON TOXIC hose for breathable air supply to the diver. The complete assembly is protected by a spiral wrap, ensuring buoyancy, organization, mechanical protection and ease of handling.
